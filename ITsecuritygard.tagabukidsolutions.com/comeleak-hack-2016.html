COMELEAK Hack 2016

  • By Kent Bayron
  • Wed 30 November 2016
March 27, 2016

A group of hacktivist widely known as "Anonymous", Anonymous Philippines claimed that they defaced the comelec website. They gain access to the root path with the root privileges by using power and appers to be an XPath injection Vuln. Anonymous Philippines request the implementation of the security features of the PCOS machines.

Hours later, The leaks has been copied. The data appears to personal informations containing names, Precint numbers and Voters identification numbers. The good thing is data is encrpted but the decryption keys were leaked.

Comelec doesn’t seems to take serious security measures, even displaying sensitive info in plain text!

April 20, 2016

The suspect was arrested in his house in Sampaloc, Manila

Defacing the Comelec Website was meant "TO GIVE VOICE TO THE VOICELESS" Paul Biteng told the Inquirer in an interview. But the data leak was not part of the plan, He added.
